Seaside Tacloban Adventist Response Team

Community Guidelines - Be faithful in serving - Be mindful and focused when in duty - Be obedient by following the rules and regulations - Be a good example to fellow responders Mission: To develop Christ-like character by serving the community within reach. Vision: To make every member a living bridge that connects people to Christ. Objective: To mold, develop, and transform each member in becoming a faithful servant of Christ.
